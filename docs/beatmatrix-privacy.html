BeatMatrix Privacy Policy

BeatMatrix does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or third-party services that collect user data.

Any data created within the app remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted externally.

No login or account information is required to use the app.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer at: jamie@jamiegabriel.org.
