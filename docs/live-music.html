Live Music

At the moment I mainly play as a solo guitarist and in small ensembles, working across a wide mix of styles. I enjoy playing standards, but also get into free improv stuff.

I am really into using effects to explores sound, texture and noise, things like abstract soundscapes. You can see more of what I’m up to on Facebook or Instagram.

I am also in the band SAJA Project, a collaboration with my partner Sally Jamila and our first album is due out in early 2026.

I love integrating geeky and techy stuff into live performance and get into things like applying real time machine models to live settings.
