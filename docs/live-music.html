Live Music

I currently perform primarily as a solo guitarist and in small ensembles, working across a wide range of styles. While standards remain central to my playing, I am equally drawn to free improvisation.

Much of my work focuses on sound, texture, and abstraction, using effects to create extended soundscapes. Updates and performance clips can be found on Facebook and Instagram.

I also perform as part of SAJA Project, a collaboration with vocalist Sally Jamila. Our debut album is scheduled for release in early 2026.

Alongside traditional performance, I explore the use of real-time computational systems and machine learning models as active participants in live improvisation.
