Wildberger's Notebooks
So I am nervous about the availability of mathematician Norman Wildberger's research being left to the mercy of YouTube! That, and I also just wanted to write up some of his amazing work to get across it. His research across Universal Hyperbolic Geometry, Rational Trigonometry and Graph Dynamics and groups is pretty mind blowing (for me at least!), so this is the page I hold to write it up. I have found Wildberger's approach to mathematics particularly helpful in challenging in rethinking mathematical approaches to Chemistry and Physics.
