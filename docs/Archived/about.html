About Me

Jamie Gabriel is a composer and data scientist dividing his time between Australia New Zealand. His background spans jazz, classical, computer science and mathematics and he studie music at the Australian Institute of Music and Sydney Convervatorium. He has extensvive experience teaching university lecturing and research and working on a wide range of software projects with a focus on AI systems.

Jamie holds a PhD in Music Information Retreival with a focus on applying machine learning models to symbolic music, and developing novel solutions for ear training and augmented listening. He has created a number of software music worked in a number of roles across Data Science, Stelupa, building musical interaces.

Jamie has alwo worked in a number of senior Data Science and AI roles across industry and government (most recently as Chief Data Officer of the Cancer Institute NSW)

Jamie is currently collaborating in SAJA recording a new album due for release in 2025.
