Overview

BeatMatrix is an iOS application designed as an interactive accompaniment and study environment for Konoko rhythm practice and modern orhextral music with lots of tempo changes.

BeatMatrix enables users to:

  • Generate and manipulate layered rhythmic cycles in real time.
  • Practice complex tempo sequences with adaptive tempo and subdivision control.
  • Visualise rhythmic relationships through dynamic pattern displays and playback modes.

Conceptually, the project draws on the rhythmic pedagogy of Rafael Reina, particularly his work on translating South Indian rhythmic concepts into contemporary compositional and improvisational frameworks. It also is a bit of a reponse to how to learn modern concert music and jazz (think Boulez, Allan Holdsworth type of stuff)

And of course it supports MIDI import and export, and relates to a bunch of other research I am doing.
